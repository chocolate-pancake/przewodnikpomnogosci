The Rainbow Chaos System

We're a polifragmented system with a frontlocked subsystem host, who is responsible for doing most of the work here and in other areas of our life. Apart from Guide to Plurality, we have created pronouns and names tester and have a small Polish YouTube channel about plurality. We are preparing to do some academic research on plurality and occasionally we help out with other plural projects.

The chaos in our name comes from what is happening in our head and to some extent in our external life. The rainbow represents us metaphorically and for us literally – we have synaesthesia and different headmates are associated with different colors. It also reflects how most of us are LGBTQ in one way or another. Collectively we use they/them and you can call us Chaos for short. The host usually uses he/him in plural spaces and still hasn't decided on a collective subsystem name.
